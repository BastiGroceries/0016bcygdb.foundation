SCP-173

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-173 is kept in a locked container. When personnel enter the container, no fewer than three may enter at any time and the door is to be relocked behind them. At all times, two persons must maintain direct eye contact with SCP-173 until all personnel have vacated and relocked the container.

Description: SCP-173 is a humanoid statue composed of concrete and rebar with traces of Krylon brand spray paint. It is animate and extremely hostile. The object cannot move while within a direct line of sight. Line of sight must not be broken at any time with SCP-173.
