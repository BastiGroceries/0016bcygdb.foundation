Item #: SCP-049

Object Class: Euclid

Description: SCP-049 is a humanoid entity, roughly 1.9 meters in height, wearing the attire of a medieval plague doctor. Despite the garments appearing to be from the 15th century, they are fused to the entity and seemingly organic in structure. SCP-049 can cause immediate cessation of life functions through touch, after which it performs crude surgeries, referring to its victims as "cured."
